Hamburger

Description:

Although the true origin of the dish is Europe, hamburger has become the most typical dish from the United States

There are a lot of different opinions on the matter of how to cook them, but if there is one thing that everybody agrees on, is that this recipe is DE-LI-CIOUS!

Ingredients:

  • 200g of grinded beef meat
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • a jar of pickles
  • american cheese
  • a brioche bread bum

Steps:

  1. Pour a couple drops of olive oil on a pan and put it on medium heat
  2. Make a ball with the grinded meat and then squish it until it becomes a fully fledged burguer patty and put it on the hot pan
  3. Cook it for 3 to 4 minutes for each side
  4. Put one slice of american cheese on top of the patty 1 minute before it is done, then put the patty inside the brioche bum
  5. Finally cut one slice of tomato, pickles and onion and put it on top of the patty
  6. Enjoy your hamburger!
